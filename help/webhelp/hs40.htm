OD to Dose conversion

measurement menu 
 
OD to Dose conversion may be used for several types of files in the Dose conversion tab, but will be used only when the menu item is checked. The algoritm used is:

 dose= a + b*OD/f + c*(OD/f)^2 + d*(OD/f)^3 + e*(OD/f)^4
